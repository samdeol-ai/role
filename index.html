About Me

I am a senior leader in the IT services and product engineering industry, focused on how AI is reshaping the way technology companies create value, build relationships, and grow revenue.

Over the years, I have seen the industry shift from project delivery to platforms, from time-and-material billing to outcome-based engagement, and from execution-led services to AI-led transformation. The leadership models that worked for the last decade will not be enough for what is coming next.

This site captures my point of view on four leadership roles I believe every modern IT services and product engineering company will need. These roles are not replacements for the existing C-suite — they are the missing connective tissue between customer, capability, industry, and AI-led growth.
