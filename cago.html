Chief AI Growth Officer

The revenue engine of the AI era — turning intelligence, capability, and customer insight into scalable growth.

Executive Summary

The Chief AI Growth Officer (CAGO) is a next-generation C-suite role designed for the AI era. It does not replace the COO, CCO, or vertical heads — it integrates them into a focused growth engine.

Build and scale AI-led revenue, and ensure the company earns a seat at the enterprise transformation table.

Role Positioning

  • COO — capability and execution
  • CCO — customer intelligence
  • EVP / Vertical Head — industry relevance
  • CAGO — AI growth orchestration

Why This Role Is Emerging

AI is moving from cost to revenue. Enterprises now expect partners who can identify use cases, build solutions, and scale across the organization. Without a CAGO, the customer pain (CCO), capability (COO) and industry relevance (EVP) signals never get converted into AI revenue at scale.

Role Philosophy

AI growth is not a function. It is a coordinated system across customer, capability, and industry.
  • AI revenue must be intentionally created, not discovered.
  • Every account must identify AI opportunities and build at least one AI-led use case.
  • Growth comes from co-creation, reuse, and scaling.

Core Responsibilities

  • AI Revenue Ownership — own AI-driven revenue across accounts; build a scalable AI pipeline.
  • Enterprise Positioning — ensure presence in client transformation programs and board-level AI conversations.
  • Use Case Engine — drive identification of AI use cases and convert them into POCs and revenue.
  • Cross-Functional Integration — align CCO insights, COO capabilities, and EVP industry knowledge.
  • Ninja Team Creation — assemble 5–6 member teams from CCO, COO and EVP organizations to drive focused initiatives.
  • Commercial Innovation — build outcome-based, agent-based, and platform-led pricing models.

Operating Model — Ninja Teams

Each team includes a Client Partner (CCO), Solution Architect (COO), Industry Lead (EVP), AI Engineer, Data/Platform expert and an optional SME. Each team must identify 1–2 AI opportunities, build one POC, and convert to revenue.

30-60-90 Day Plan

First 30 Days

Identify & Align

  • Identify top 20 accounts
  • Map AI maturity
  • Create initial AI opportunity list
  • Form first ninja teams
Next 60 Days

Build & Pilot

  • Launch 5–10 POCs
  • Build AI use case repository
  • Enable CRO with propositions
Next 90 Days

Scale & Monetize

  • Convert POCs to deals
  • Replicate across accounts
  • Launch AI offerings
  • Track AI revenue growth

KPIs

AI Revenue (Primary)

  • Total AI-driven revenue and % of revenue from AI
  • AI pipeline value
  • POC-to-deal conversion rate

Account Penetration

  • # accounts with AI programs and executive engagement
  • # accounts in transformation agenda

Innovation

  • # use cases identified and POCs launched
  • # POCs converted and reusable solutions

OKRs

Build AI Revenue Engine

  • 25% revenue from AI
  • 20+ POCs launched
  • 50% POC-to-deal conversion

Enter Enterprise Transformation Agenda

  • Presence in top 10 client programs
  • 2 CXO engagements per key account

Build Scalable AI Solutions

  • 10 reusable AI solutions
  • 5 cross-account replications

Final Positioning

The Chief AI Growth Officer is the revenue engine of the AI era — turning intelligence, capability, and customer insight into scalable growth.
